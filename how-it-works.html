How SongRaise Works for Charities

SongRaise helps nonprofits launch fundraising campaigns powered by music. Each campaign gets a shareable page with a song and a donate button.

  • A fundraising song tailored to your cause, condition, and tone.
  • Optional lyrics for transparency and accessibility.
